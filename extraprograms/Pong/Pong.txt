Pong:

Based on the computer game pong
Player 1 is on the left, player 2 is on the right. Works best if seated next to each other
Player 1 serves first
Pressing A serves the ball towards you
Press A to move the bat up, and B to move the bat down
Ball will move to opponent's microbit if it reaches the edge of the screen
Opponent gains a point if you fail to hit the ball
The loser of that round serves next
First to 5 points wins
